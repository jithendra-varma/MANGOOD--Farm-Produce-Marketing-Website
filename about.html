About

Our Village K.B.R.Puram.

Mango is also known as the king of all fruits,is also the national fruit of our country. For a certain fruit to be declared as a national fruit, it should have certain characteristics. First of all, it should represent the complete country i.e. India. The different varieties of mango represent the different cultures, societies, casts, races and mindsets in India. It is a symbol of cultural diversity.

The main idea behind the website is to show the world the varieties of mangoes which we produce in farms of our village that show cases our cultural diversity. Our village Kumara Bomma Raja Puram simply called as K.B.R.Puram or Adavi Kandriga. K.B.R.Puram is located in the Chittoor District of Andhra Pradesh in India.

Before learning about our village let me tell you its speciality. We know india is the largest producer of mangoes. In India, Uttar pradesh takes the first place with 24% of the total production of mangoes. Second place goes to Andhra Pradesh with 22% of the total production of mangoes. In Andhra Pradesh, Chittoor district is leading in mango production with area under cultivation being 77637ha and with production of 854007t of mangoes per year.

In the production of mangoes our village is a small part of chittoor district contributing over 2000t of mangoes per year with area under cultivation being 1500 acres approx. In our village we grow lots of varieties over which the top 10 varieties being Alphonso, Sindhura, Totapuri, Neelum, Rumani, Banganapalli, Himayat, Malgoa, Javari.
